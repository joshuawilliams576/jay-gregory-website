About Jay

A lifelong martial artist, on and off the mats

Jay Harley Gregory is a BJJ purple belt and active competitor on the AJP Tour, with 11+ years of experience in MMA and a background spanning boxing, kickboxing and grappling. Fluent in both English and Spanish, he coaches members and competitors alike with the same technical, disciplined approach he brings to his own training.

A Level 3 Personal Trainer, Level 3 Fitness & Wellness Trainer and qualified Nutritionist, Jay coaches Boxing, Powerwave, MMA, BJJ and Circuit training out of Bulldog Gym Tenerife — working with everyone from complete beginners to competitors preparing for their own tournaments.
